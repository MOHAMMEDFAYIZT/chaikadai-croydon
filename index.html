Welcome to Chai Kadai Croydon

Bold Flavours | Hearty Portions | True Comfort

Step into Chai Kadai Croydon, where every meal brings people together over rich flavours and comforting spices. From our signature Mandi rice loaded with tender meat, to crispy fried chicken, freshly cooked fish, and an exciting range of street-style snacks, our menu is made to satisfy every craving.

Every dish at Chai Kadai is prepared with care, using quality ingredients and traditional recipes that capture the spirit of homestyle cooking. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a feast with friends and family, you'll find the perfect balance of taste and warmth right here in the heart of Croydon.

Come hungry. Leave happy.
